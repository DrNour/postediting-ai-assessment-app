# Pilot Study Design

## Aim

To evaluate whether AI-generated post-editing feedback is accurate, useful, and pedagogically appropriate when reviewed by translation teachers.

## Suggested dataset

- 30-50 post-edited segments
- 1-2 teacher reviewers
- AI feedback for each segment
- Teacher approve/edit/reject decision
- Usefulness rating from 1 to 5
- Teacher review time

## Suggested research questions

1. To what extent do AI-generated error categories agree with teacher-assigned categories?
2. How often do teachers approve, edit, or reject AI-generated feedback?
3. How useful do teachers perceive AI-generated feedback to be?
4. What types of feedback require the most teacher editing?

## Suggested analyses

- Approval/edit/reject frequencies and percentages
- Cohen's kappa for AI-teacher category agreement
- Classification report for AI categories
- Mean and standard deviation of usefulness ratings
- Qualitative analysis of teacher notes
